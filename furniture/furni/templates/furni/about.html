Our Story

Founded with a passion for stylish and functional living, We bring thoughtfully designed pieces to homes across India. At Module Furniture, we blend tradition with innovation, offering a diverse range of high-quality furniture solutions for every space. As a proud subsidiary of the well-established Thayyil Furniture—a name trusted by households and businesses for decades—we bring a fresh, modern perspective to the industry. Our mission is to cater to the evolving needs of our customers with versatile designs that enhance both comfort and functionality. Whether you're looking to transform your living room, upgrade your dining area, furnish your office, or create a stylish ambiance in hotels, Module Furniture has a wide selection to meet your requirements. Each piece is crafted with the same dedication to quality and detail that has been a hallmark of Thayyil Furniture, now infused with contemporary design elements that reflect today’s trends. With an emphasis on sustainability and customer satisfaction, we provide tailored solutions for every lifestyle. From classic to modern, minimalist to bold, Module Furniture ensures your spaces are as unique and dynamic as you are. Welcome to the future of furniture—where tradition meets modernity.

Our Mission

At Module Furniture, our mission is to make high-quality, affordable furniture accessible to everyone—blending craftsmanship, comfort, and modern aesthetics. We strive to create pieces that not only elevate everyday living but also reflect the unique personalities and lifestyles of our customers. By combining time-tested traditions with forward-thinking design, we aim to deliver versatile furniture solutions that inspire, endure, and adapt to the way you live today.
