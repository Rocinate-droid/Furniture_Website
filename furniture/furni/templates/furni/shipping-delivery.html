Shipping and Delivery

You can check the delivery timelines by entering your pin code at product page.

Furniture products will be shipped via our own Logistics within 7–8 days. After shipping, the product will be delivered in 10–15 days. In case of Module’s logistics, one day prior a delivery confirmation call will be undertaken by our executive.
You can also reach out to us at modulefurnitures@gmail.com or call us on +91 8129718086.

Assembly of product will be undertaken within 3 to 5 days after delivery.

We offer FREE SHIPPING for our furniture range on selected pincodes and within certain price ranges. Otherwise, a Rs.999 shipping charge is applicable.

To know if delivery is available in your area, enter your pin code on the product page. We are currently servicing only Alapuzha, Thrissur, and Ernakulam districts in Kerala. For other locations, respective courier charges shall apply.

In case you book multiple products in a single order, we will endeavour to ship all products together. However, this may not always be possible due to the nature of the product and/or the mode of delivery.

Module’s delivery executive will share a confirmation call one day before delivery. In case of non-availability, kindly inform the delivery executive.

Post placing the order, you can change the delivery address if the product is not shipped, subject to the new delivery address being in the same city.

We only deliver to the address specified in the order. In case of delivery to multiple addresses, you must place separate orders for each location.

Whoever is accepting the delivery on your behalf needs to have a valid ID proof.
